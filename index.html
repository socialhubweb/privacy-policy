Overview

Social Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

📥

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name and email address
  • Profile photo (optional, if you choose to upload)
  • Platform credentials you choose to save (stored securely)

We also automatically collect:

  • Daily screen time usage within the app
  • Number of times each platform is opened
  • App settings and preferences
⚙️

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Social Hub account
  • To provide auto-fill for saved platform credentials
  • To show you personalized stats and achievements
  • To send app notifications and engagement reminders
  • To improve app performance and features
🌐

3. Third-Party Platforms

Social Hub uses WebView technology to display third-party platforms including Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Gemini, Daraz, X (Twitter), Tamasha, Messenger, and LinkedIn.

When you use these platforms inside our app, each platform's own privacy policy applies to your activity. We do not intercept or store your activity on these platforms.
  • Cookies and sessions are stored locally on your device for login persistence
  • We do not read your messages or posts on third-party platforms
🗄️

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore):

  • Accessible only by you — no other user can view your data
  • Protected by strict Firebase security rules
  • Stored on Google's secure, encrypted cloud servers
🔔

5. Notifications

Social Hub sends local and push notifications to remind you to check your social platforms. You can disable notifications anytime from your device settings or within the app under Settings → Notifications.

🚫

6. We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal data to any third party
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Access your messages or content on third-party platforms
  • Store your platform passwords in plain text
  • Track your location
👦

7. Children's Privacy

Social Hub is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

✅

8. Your Rights

  • Access and update your info via the Profile screen
  • Delete your account and all data via Profile → Delete Account
  • Opt out of notifications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us
🕐

9. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all your data — including stats, credentials, and notifications — is permanently and immediately deleted from our servers.

📝

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
